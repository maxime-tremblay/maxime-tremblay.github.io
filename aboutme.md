My name is **Maxime Tremblay**. I've been working with Oracle APEX since 2008, from version 2.x through everything it's become today, building applications across a wide range of industries and company sizes. Based in Québec, Canada, I've had the chance to work directly with the Oracle APEX team over the years, and I also architect and deploy full Oracle Cloud Infrastructure stacks when a project calls for it.

I'm active in the APEX community. I've answered over 1,000 questions on the [apex.world](https://apex.world) Slack, was awarded Member of the Year in 2016, and have spoken at Kscope, APEX@Home, and Oracle Developer Day. This blog is part of the same effort: things that come up in real projects, written down so others don't have to figure them out from scratch.

I'm also the founder of [askMax](https://askmax.solutions), where I offer professional APEX support, training, and coaching.
